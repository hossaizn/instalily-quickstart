Primrose & Eve June 2026 · live on Apple App Store App Store →

Doctor-visit advocacy app for perimenopause. Solo-built, end to end.

Symptoms tracked entirely on-device. Statistical Pattern Engine runs locally to surface correlations. On-demand Smart Report compresses ninety days of data into three clear asks for the doctor. No central database of user data exists, by architecture, which is a defensible privacy claim in a category that usually fails on this dimension. Solo-shipped through Apple App Review. Stack: Swift, SwiftUI, StoreKit 2, Cloudflare Workers backend, on-device AI report generation, DeviceCheck for abuse prevention.
